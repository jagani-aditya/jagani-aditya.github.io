About Me

I recently graduated from K.J. Somaiya College of Engineering, Mumbai, India, and obtained a bachelor's degree in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering.

I am currently interning at Research Innovation Incubation Design Labs (RIIDL), where I collaborated with other Makers to build a Humanoid Robot from scratch under the direct supervision of Mr. Gaurang Shetty, CEO/ Chief Innovation Catalyst of RIIDL (Marked 50 Most Inspiring CEO's by Silicon India).

In association with The Marine Robotics Team (TMRT), I am developing navigation techniques for an Autonomous Underwater Vehicle using Finite State Machines for the SAUVC Competition held in Singapore in April 2020.

My current interests lie in designing and building robotic solutions for complex, unpredictable, and continuously changing environments through robust control systems. I love field robotics and testing robots in uncertain environments!!
