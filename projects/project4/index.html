Project 4 Name

For my senior year project, I designed, machined, integrated and developed software for a Tactical IoT based Surveillance Bot. The bot was carefully designed to climb stairs. It had an on-board firearm (toy gun) which could be controlled over the net from a webpage from any authenticated remote desktop using a mouse. Moving the mouse horizontally or vertically would move the firearm accordingly, just like in a first-person shooting game. Keyboard controls were used to control the locomotion, speed of the robot, while also toggling laser and torch lights. Check out the SolidWorks design as well as a short demo here!!
