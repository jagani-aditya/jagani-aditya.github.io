Humanoid Robot

The Humanoid Robot is a 3D printed life-size robot whose design is similar to the Inmoov. The primary aim of developing the humanoid robot was to impart the physical as well as the mental traits of a human being. For instance, we imbibed perception algorithms that can not only recognize humans, maintain eye contact with them but also respond well while conversing with a human.
